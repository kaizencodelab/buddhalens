About Buddhalens

This website exists to preserve and clarify oral transmission. Its purpose is to provide a secure, structured space where practitioners can record the oral instructions they have personally received from a qualified teacher, and accurately trace the lineage of those teachings—where they originated, how they were transmitted, and through whom they were passed down. By documenting both the content of a transmission and its lineage, the platform helps maintain authenticity, context, and continuity across generations. It is not a replacement for direct instruction, but a supporting record that honors the teacher–student relationship and the unbroken chain through which these teachings are meant to flow. The goal is simple: preserve accuracy, protect lineage, and ensure that oral teachings are remembered as they were given.
